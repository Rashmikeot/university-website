Location of Sikkim University

Sikkim University is currently located at Gangtok, the capital town of Sikkim. The University is at present operating from several hired buildings spread over 17 kms along the National Highway - 10. The main administrative office of the University is located at Samdur, 6th Mile, Tadong. The permanent campus site of the University is located at Yangang in South Sikkim. The nearest airport is at Bagdogra and the nearest railway station is at New Jalpaiguri.
